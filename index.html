About

A builder first, with the judgment of someone who has shipped at scale.

I started out as a freelance developer during my undergrad, shipping small sites and apps for real clients. Years as a software engineer and tech lead followed, and I watched genAI collapse the gap between an idea and a working prototype.

I have leaned into that shift on purpose, building products throughout my Oxford MBA. The value is not just speed. Every product forces real judgment calls on what to prioritise, what to cut, where the friction sits and when something is good enough to put in front of people.

My foundation is engineering at scale. That means I treat AI as a tool with real constraints and failure modes, I design around them and I can evaluate whether something actually works rather than just demos well.
